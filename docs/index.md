## Features

<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Lightning-fast access</h3>
    <p>Search your saved folders from Command Palette (Win + Alt + Space) and open them instantly in any terminal.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Your preferred terminals</h3>
    <p>Works with Windows Terminal, PowerShell, WSL, Git Bash, and any terminal profile on your PC.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Auto-run commands</h3>
    <p>Automatically execute dev servers, build scripts, or setup commands when opening a folder.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Home screen shortcuts</h3>
    <p>Create home keywords to jump directly to your most-used projects without searching.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>Backup and sync</h3>
    <p>Export and import shortcuts to back up your setup or move it to another PC in seconds.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3>100% private</h3>
    <p>All shortcuts and settings stay on your PC. No cloud sync, no account, no telemetry.</p>
  </div>
</div>

## Requirements

Quick Shell requires:

- **Windows 10** version 2004 or later (Windows 11 recommended)
- **PowerToys** with [Command Palette](https://learn.microsoft.com/windows/powertoys/install) enabled
